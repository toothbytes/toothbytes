Toothbytes is a dental management software exclusively only for Dr. Ruben Pascual Dental Clinic. This software helps the Dr. Pascual with the organization of the patients’ personal and dental records which includes the management of the billing records and the appointment scheduling. For more information, please visit Pines City Doctors' Hospital, Baguio City, Benguet, Philippines.
